A Bit About Us

Our Story

Simply Granola began in our family kitchen with a passion for creating nutritious, delicious breakfast options. What started as a weekend ritual quickly became a local favorite at our neighborhood farmers' market.

Today, we remain committed to our original values: simple, wholesome ingredients combined with thoughtful recipes to create granola that's simply delicious.

Nutrition

We believe that good food should nourish both body and soul. That's why every batch of Simply Granola is crafted with nutrient-dense ingredients like whole grain oats, nuts rich in healthy fats, and natural sweeteners.
